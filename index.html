About

I'm Nihal — a marketer who builds, not just manages.

I spent 4+ years building marketing systems for brands that had nothing — no team, no audience, no playbook. I built the team, the strategy, and the system. I've done this across D2C, hospitality, and edtech, in India and globally.

In 2024 I founded Axiom & Co. — a consultancy that delivers full-funnel marketing to founders who don't have time to figure it out. And right now I'm building Handelio — an AI receptionist that handles leads for small businesses on WhatsApp, Instagram, and Telegram. I understand growth from both sides of the table.

I'm open to senior growth marketing roles — remote-first, India or Australia. If you're building something ambitious and need someone who can run the entire marketing function, let's talk.

Case 01 · August 2025 – April 2026

Devi Enterprises — Building a multi-brand growth engine from zero.

Problem

Four new D2C brands. No marketing team, no audience, no system in place to acquire or retain customers.

Approach

Built a 15-person cross-functional team from scratch. Designed full-funnel growth strategy. Managed Meta Ads (~₹1L/month). Built a 500+ global influencer network across India, UAE, USA, Iran, and Nepal. Led SEO and website development.

Results
  • Promolecules: 0 → 16K followers
  • Earthmaa: 0 → 14K followers
  • Madbeef: 0 → 6K followers
  • Grainly: 0 → 6K followers
  • 15-person team built and operational
Case 02 · 2024–Present

Axiom & Co. — AI-driven marketing for hospitality brands.

Problem

Approach
